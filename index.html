About

The Surface Laptop (1st Gen) combines elegant design and reliable performance. With an ultra-slim aluminum body, Alcantara® keyboard, and a vibrant 13.5" PixelSense™ display, it delivers a premium experience for students and professionals. Designed for portability and productivity, it pairs a lightweight chassis with long battery life and a comfortable typing experience.

Specifications

Performance

  • Processor: 7th Gen Intel® Core™ i5 / i7
  • Memory: 4GB / 8GB / 16GB
  • Storage: 128GB / 256GB / 512GB / 1TB SSD

Display & Media

  • 13.5” PixelSense™ Display (2256 × 1504)
  • Omnisonic speakers with Dolby® Audio™ Premium
  • 720p HD front-facing camera

Battery & Power

  • Battery Life: Up to 14.5 hours (video playback)
  • Surface Connect port, efficient power delivery

Ports & Connectivity

  • USB-A, Mini DisplayPort, Surface Connect, 3.5mm audio
  • Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, Bluetooth 4.0
  • Security: TPM, Windows Hello facial sign-in
